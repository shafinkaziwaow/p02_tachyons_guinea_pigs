Polygonal Derby


Welcome to the parody of the hit rhythm game Geometry Dash! Travel through the world of polygons to reach your destiny!


Instructions/Controls

Avoid colliding with the objects in the course. You may not jump onto a spike. You may not crash horizontall into a wall.

Press jump on yellow orbs to jump in the air.

Press jump on blue orbs to be sent straight down.

To jump use either up arrow, space, or click the screen.

Green portals activate cube mode. You can only jump on the ground and while using orbs.

Purple portals activate ship mode. You can hold or tap jump in the air to fly.

Orange portals activate UFO mode. You can jump in the air.
